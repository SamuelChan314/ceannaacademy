What we can help with

School Research

Compare public, Catholic, private, and post-secondary options based on location, grade level, program needs, and family preferences.

Application Preparation

Prepare school application materials, check requirements, organize deadlines, and communicate with schools where applicable.

Document Organization

Create checklists and organize study-related documents so families understand what is needed and when.

Arrival Planning

Support planning around homestay, custodian needs, school communication, and transition questions where applicable.

Important service boundary

Ceanna Academy provides academic consulting, school application support, and document organization. Canadian immigration advice or paid immigration representation must be provided by an authorized licensed professional. When a task requires regulated immigration advice or representation, we can help families understand the next step and work alongside the appropriate authorized professional.
